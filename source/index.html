What is a VPN?

A VPN, or virtual private network, will redirect your Internet connection through a secure tunnel, hide your IP address, and encrypt your personal data. A VPN gives you online privacy and protects against surveillance, interference, and censorship. The internet is inherently dangerous, and anyone can fall victim to cybercriminals. But you can protect yourself by building a digital fortress around your online business. With a VPN, you can also safely watch your favorite foreign movies and TV shows online, wherever you are.
